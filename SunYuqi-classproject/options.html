Units in the school: According to the needs of the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia epidemic, the school decided to adjust some of the New Year's Day holidays in 2021 to the winter vacation. The specific arrangements are as follows.

On January 1, 2021 (Friday), New Year's Day will be closed for one day, and on January 2 and 3 (Saturday and Sunday), final exams and work will start. Winter vacation will start on January 7 (Thursday).
